Frank M Ligons

Bestselling author and ELECTRIFYING Speaker

Having endured decades of incapacitating anxiety and depression, Frank M. Ligons understands the burdens of chronic illness and disability. After trying dozens of medications, he finally discovered ketamine therapy.

Ketamine stopped his 25 years of suicidal thoughts in their tracks!
